Welcome to Steward

A few things to know before you begin

Storage Setup Required

Your uploaded receipts need a destination to be stored. Please configure your storage settings — choose between local filesystem or your own AWS S3 bucket. Until configured, files fall back to the server's default upload directory.

Your Privacy

We do not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way. Your receipts and personal information are stored securely and used solely to power the features of this app — spending analytics, cashback insights, and receipt management. We do not use your data for advertising or any third-party analytics platforms. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Legal Disclaimer

Steward is a personal finance and asset management tool intended for individual use. It does not constitute financial, tax, or legal advice. The analytics and cashback estimates shown are for reference only and may not reflect actual card terms or tax obligations. Always consult a qualified professional for financial or tax decisions.
